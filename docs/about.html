It all started with a Goldendoodle...

...with the face of a teddy and the bark of a bear...

Golden Bear Studios is named after Lucy, my Mini Goldendoodle, who, like all good dogs, has been my light and grounding force (all whilst looking consistently adorable).

After studying Fine Art at university, I threw myself into the digital design industry. But years in the corporate world eventually took their toll, leaving me creatively drained and questioning my priorities. The higher I climbed, the further I felt from what mattered most - balance, creativity, and time with loved ones. Something had to give.

Golden Bear Studios is my way back to what I love: creating work that’s thoughtful, hands-on, and true to my values. Lucy’s unfiltered joy keeps me honest, and I aim to bring that same authenticity to every project, crafting designs that are as genuine and heartfelt as my early days in the studio, now sharpened by both an artist’s eye and a designer’s approach.
